Airwallex AgentOS is a toolkit that lets AI agents manage financial operations through plain-language prompts, executing instructions like billing a customer, paying invoices, or reconciling transactions end to end. Plugins are available today on Claude Code, Claude Cowork, and Cursor, and it works with any agent environment.
