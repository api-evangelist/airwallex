AI agents can triage bugs, generate code, and ship fixes to production, yet most organizations continue using outdated organizational structures and handoff processes designed when engineering was the primary bottleneck. The article examines how forward-looking product teams are restructuring their workflows and team designs to match what AI-augmented development actually enables.
